ABOUT US

We care for the children, Join us ..

Care For Children is a non-profit organization. We raise funds to provide daily meals to poor children. We also have accomodation facilities and are expanding more across the country. Our next goal is to support these children for their basic education.

Services

know how we care about the children

WE PLAN

We have arranged daily meals across all our bootcamps and centers for the needy and poor children.

WE BUILD

We have built 30+ infrastructure projects for these children including 4 schools, 7 orphanages, 16 residential buildings supporting 2000+ children and poor people and also maintain 300+ bootcamps which serve food to 10000+ children, thanks to the support of individual donors and sponsor institutions.

WE PROMOTE

We make sure that all our children receive the best education and also learning expenses of books, stationary, school uniforms are taken care off.
